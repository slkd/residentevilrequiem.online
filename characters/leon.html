1. The Missing Officer: Leon's Investigation

Set roughly 30 years after the events of Raccoon City, Leon is far from the naive rookie cop from 1998. In Resident Evil Requiem, he operates at the highest echelons of federal counter-bioterrorism.

Leon is dispatched to the isolated, abandoned hotel in the American Midwest following the disappearance of a fellow law enforcement officer who was looking into a string of bizarre, occult-like deaths. His investigation path directly crosses with Grace Ashcroft, the daughter of Alyssa Ashcroft from the beloved Outbreak series.

2. High-Octane RE4-Style Action

As confirmed by professional review threads and Reddit consensuses, Capcom has built Requiem on a dual-protagonist structure. But unlike classic RE2 where both characters play similarly, Grace represents extreme vulnerability, while Leon Resident Evil Requiem sequences represent extreme competence.

  • Parry System: The immensely popular knife parry system from the RE4 Remake returns. Leon can deflect chainsaws, redirect thrown weapons, and break out of grapples instantly.
  • Roundhouse Kicks (Melee): Stunning a zombie or B.O.W. with a quick handgun shot to the knee or face opens them up for Leon's signature devastating melee prompts.
  • Arsenal: Leon starts his campaign with much heavier firepower compared to Grace, bringing military-grade shotguns, tactical SMGs, and eventually finding the iconic Lightning Hawk Magnum.

Critics have highlighted that playing as Leon feels like "venting frustration" after surviving Grace's harrowing, stealth-focused terrifying segments.
