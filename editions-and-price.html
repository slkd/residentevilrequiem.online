Is the Resident Evil Requiem Deluxe Edition Worth It?

If you are debating whether to upgrade, you need to consider the Resident Evil Requiem price bump versus the value of the included DLC.

The standard edition is priced at the modern industry baseline of $69.99 USD across all platforms, including the Nintendo Switch 2. The Resident Evil Requiem Deluxe Edition comes in at $89.99 USD. For that extra $20, you are primarily paying for the nostalgia and aesthetic variations.

If you are a fan of Leon S. Kennedy or appreciate the long-running lore connecting Grace to Alyssa Ashcroft from Outbreak, the extra costumes provide excellent replayability value. The "Found Footage" screen filter alone is expected to completely change the terrifying atmosphere of the abandoned hotel.

Pre-Order Bonuses Explained

No matter which edition you choose, reserving your copy before February 27, 2026, grants you the following digital bonuses:

  • Survival Resource Pack: A bundle of early-game herbs, handgun ammo, and a lockpick, deposited into your first safe room item box.
  • Classic Alyssa Costume: A nostalgic throwback outfit for Grace, mirroring her mother's signature red jacket from Resident Evil Outbreak.

Note: If you plan to pursue the Trophy & Achievement roadmap, the Survival Resource Pack can provide a slight edge during your initial "Hardcore" difficulty playthrough.
