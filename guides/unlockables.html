🏆 Completionist Guide: Secrets & Weapons

Resident Evil Requiem Unlockables Guide

Replayability has always been the cornerstone of the franchise, and Resident Evil Requiem is no exception. After surviving the Midwest Hotel Incident for the first time, a massive array of post-game content becomes available. This guide details exactly how to earn those coveted Resident Evil Requiem unlockables, including infinite ammo weapons and secret costumes.

Intel Briefing

  • 1. How to Unlock Infinite Ammo Weapons
  • 2. Secret Alternate Costumes
  • 3. The Mercenaries Mode

1. How to Unlock Infinite Ammo Weapons

If you felt Grace's survival horror sections were too stressful due to a lack of resources, Revenge is a dish best served with infinite bullets. To unlock the ultimate power-fantasies for subsequent playthroughs, you must meet stringent completion criteria.

Weapon Name Unlock Condition
Infinite Combat Knife (Leon only) Destroy all 16 hidden "Mr. Raccoon" statues tucked away throughout the hotel.
Samurai Edge (Handgun - Infinite Ammo) Complete the game on "Standard" difficulty or higher with an S Rank.
Chicago Sweeper (Submachine Gun - Infinite Ammo) Complete the game on "Hardcore" difficulty with an S Rank.
Handcannon (Magnum - Infinite Ammo) Complete a New Game (no New Game Plus imports) on the punishing "Insanity" difficulty without utilizing any bonus weapons.

2. Secret Alternate Costumes

Alongside the outfits provided in the Resident Evil Requiem Deluxe Edition, dedicated players can unlock humorous and nostalgic costumes via the Extra Content Shop using Completion Points (CP) earned from in-game challenges.

  • Leon's RPD Uniform (1998): Earned by completing the campaign under 8 hours.
  • Grace's "Investigator" Suit: Earned by collecting 100% of the in-game lore files and documents.
  • Retro Polygon Models (PS1 Style): Available in the Extra Content Shop for 20,000 CP after finishing the game on any difficulty.

3. The Mercenaries Mode

As confirmed by early reviews, the beloved arcade-style The Mercenaries minigame makes its triumphant return. It is unlocked automatically upon your first clear of the main campaign on any difficulty.

You initially start with Leon S. Kennedy. Achieving an "A" Rank on the first two stages (Lobby and Underground Facility) will unlock Grace Ashcroft, equipped with a uniquely rapid trap-setting loadout. Rumors within the Reddit community heavily suggest Ada Wong will be added as a playable Mercenaries character in a free post-launch update.
