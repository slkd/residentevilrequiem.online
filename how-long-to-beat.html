How Long to Beat Resident Evil Requiem?

As the massive launch of Capcom's highly anticipated survival horror title approaches, one of the most frequently asked questions is: "Resident Evil Requiem how long to beat?" Thanks to early Metacritic reviews and Reddit impressions, we now have a highly accurate breakdown of exactly how many hours you can expect to spend navigating the horrors of the Midwest hotel.

Is the Game Too Short?

A 10 to 12-hour main campaign places Resident Evil Requiem squarely in the perfect "sweet spot" identified by long-time fans. It is slightly shorter than the sprawling Resident Evil 4 Remake (which averaged 15+ hours), but notably longer than Resident Evil 3 Remake.

As noted broadly by the Reddit community: "It sounds like a perfect sweetspot to me. Doesn't overstay its welcome."

Remember, the franchise is built on heavy replayability. Achieving an "S-Rank" on Hardcore or Insanity difficulty encourages multiple playthroughs. Furthermore, the inclusion of The Mercenaries arcade mode adds endless hours of arcade-style combat after the credits roll.
