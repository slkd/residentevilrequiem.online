⚠️ Ray Tracing Note

Ray tracing requires an NVIDIA RTX or AMD RX 6000 series GPU or newer. DLSS 3 Frame Generation requires RTX 40 series. If your GPU doesn't support ray tracing, the game runs in standard rasterization mode with no performance impact.
