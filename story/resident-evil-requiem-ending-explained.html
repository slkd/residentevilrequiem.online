📖 Lore Deep Dive: The Midwest Hotel

Resident Evil Requiem Ending Explained

🚨 MASSIVE SPOILER WARNING 🚨

This article discusses the final acts, boss fights, and plot twists of Resident Evil Requiem. If you have not completed both Grace and Leon's campaigns, turn back now.

Following a harrowing 12-hour journey through the decaying corridors of the Midwest Hotel, players are left with jaw-dropping revelations regarding Umbrella's lingering shadow, Alyssa Ashcroft's tragic fate, and Leon S. Kennedy's unwavering commitment to stopping bioterrorism. Here is a comprehensive breakdown of the Resident Evil Requiem ending.

Intel Briefing

  • 1. The Secret of the Abandoned Hotel
  • 2. Grace Ashcroft's Choice
  • 3. How the Ending Sets Up Resident Evil 10

1. The Secret of the Abandoned Hotel

Throughout the game, we learn that the hotel wasn't simply a random location for an outbreak. It served as a covert, off-the-books relay station for former Umbrella executives funneling B.O.W. research data out of the United States following the Raccoon City incident nearly 30 years ago.

The "occult-like" deaths that drew both Grace and Leon to the site were the result of a dormant, highly unstable strain of a prototype virus (code-named REQUIEM) finally mutating beyond the facility's decaying containment protocols. Alyssa Ashcroft discovered this truth eight years prior, which led directly to her assassination.

2. Grace Ashcroft's Choice and the Final Battle

The climax converges brilliantly, merging Grace's terrifying survival horror gameplay with Leon's heavy firepower. In the depths of the hotel's underground laboratory, Grace finally uncovers her mother's final audio logs, granting her the closure she desperately sought.

During the final boss fight—a grotesque amalgamation of the REQUIEM virus and the remaining corrupt researchers—Grace is forced to initiate the facility's self-destruct sequence manually while Leon suppresses the creature. The ending cinematic beautifully encapsulates their dynamic: Leon, the hardened veteran, shielding Grace, the analytical survivor, as the hotel collapses into a sinkhole of fire.

3. How the Ending Sets Up Resident Evil 10

The post-credits scene is what truly has the Reddit community buzzing in the review threads. As Leon and Grace are extracted via helicopter, a seemingly innocuous briefcase retrieved by Leon is handed off to a shadowy figure—widely speculated by fans to be a contact tied to Ada Wong or a newly established anti-bioterrorism coalition.

Furthermore, Grace officially declines Leon's offer to join his elite federal task force. Instead, she chooses to publish her mother's completed manuscript, effectively blowing the lid off the newly exposed bioweapon black market to the global public. This ending masterfully closes the 30-year Raccoon City loop while propelling the franchise into a drastically new, public-facing geopolitical conflict for the inevitable Resident Evil 10.
